Who We Are

Rayyan Resources is a next-generation mineral exploration and mining services company committed to unlocking the geological potential of Saudi Arabia and frontier markets.

Headquartered in the Kingdom, we operate with a strong regional presence and international partnerships across Canada, China, and East Africa. What sets us apart is our strategic focus: we don't just offer services — we build value.

By combining advanced geoscience, innovative technology, and cross-border expertise, Rayyan Resources positions itself as a platform for long-term exploration value creation, supporting clients from early-stage mapping to mine development and investment readiness.

  • Integrity – We act transparently, ethically, and responsibly.

  • Innovation – We adopt the latest exploration technologies to drive discovery.

Integrated Services

We offer end-to-end exploration and mining services designed to enhance project valuation from the earliest stages to investment readiness.

Geological Mapping & Exploration

Regional and detailed geological surveys (1:100k to 1:5,000), alteration/structural mapping, and target generation via QGIS/QField platforms.

Remote Sensing & GIS Analysis

Satellite-based mineral targeting (ASTER, Sentinel), landform interpretation, and 3D terrain modeling for risk and access planning.

Geophysical Surveys

Ground magnetic, IP/Resistivity, VLF, gravity, radiometric, and borehole surveys with full data acquisition, processing, and interpretation.

Drilling & Core Management

Supervision of RC, Diamond, Air Core, and Auger drilling. Includes planning, core management, QA/QC, and partner coordination.

Geochemical Sampling & Assay

Rock chip, soil, and sediment sampling programs with sample tracking and management through accredited laboratory partnerships.

Resource Estimation & Modeling

JORC/NI 43-101 compliant geological/grade modeling, 3D block modeling, variography, and resource estimation using Leapfrog & Surpac.

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to protecting the environments we work in — both for today and for future generations.

  • Minimal surface disturbance: We design exploration programs that limit clearing and excavation.
  • Site rehabilitation: After drilling, we restore sites to their natural state in line with regulatory standards.
  • Remote and drone-based surveys: Where possible, we use drone technology to reduce the need for physical access and lower our impact.
