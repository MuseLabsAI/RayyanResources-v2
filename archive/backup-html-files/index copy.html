Integrated Services

We offer end-to-end exploration and mining services designed to enhance project valuation from the earliest stages to investment readiness.

Geological Mapping & Exploration

Regional and detailed geological surveys (1:100k to 1:5,000), alteration/structural mapping, and target generation via QGIS/QField platforms.

Remote Sensing & GIS Analysis

Satellite-based mineral targeting (ASTER, Sentinel), landform interpretation, and 3D terrain modeling for risk and access planning.

Geophysical Surveys

Ground magnetic, IP/Resistivity, VLF, gravity, radiometric, and borehole surveys with full data acquisition, processing, and interpretation.

Drilling & Core Management

Supervision of RC, Diamond, Air Core, and Auger drilling. Includes planning, core management, QA/QC, and partner coordination.

Geochemical Sampling & Assay

Rock chip, soil, and sediment sampling programs with sample tracking and management through accredited laboratory partnerships.

Investment Readiness & Support

JV/licensing support, data room setup for capital raising, ESG alignment, and technical strategy for asset valuation growth.

Resource Estimation & Modeling

JORC/NI 43-101 compliant geological/grade modeling, 3D block modeling, variography, and resource estimation using Leapfrog & Surpac.
